About the Platform

Property management

P-rent is a web-based apartment management platform designed to simplify communication between landlords and tenants across Kenya.

Tenants can check rent balances, view payment history, and monitor utility bills from anywhere. Landlords manage rooms, apartments, and tenant records efficiently — without paperwork.
